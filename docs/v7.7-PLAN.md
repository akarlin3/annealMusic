# AnnealMusic v7.7 Closeout Plan (v7.7-PLAN.md)

This document establishes the architecture, outline, and delivery plan for closing the **v7 Research Collaboration Arc** (v7.0 → v7.7).

---

## 1. Document Structure for Community-Specific Bundles

We are delivering two specialized, comprehensive onboarding and publication guide bundles in `docs/community/`:

### A. ICAD (Sonification) Researchers (`docs/community/icad/`)

- **`GETTING_STARTED.md`**: Guide for domain scientists (astrophysics, ecology, network security) to translate continuous/discrete variables into high-fidelity real-time audio.
- **`RECIPES.md`**: Curated configurations of v7.3 sonification templates tailored for auditory graphing, model-based sonifications, and multi-dimensional coordinate sweeps.
- **`PUBLISHING.md`**: Detailed workflow to embed the Vanilla HTML5 iframe widget (`/embed-figure`), export BibTeX references, and produce H.264 MP4 video abstracts for journals.
- **`CASE_STUDIES.md`**: Three detailed anonymized studies representing space physics, continuous ecological monitors, and real-time network threat monitoring via Kuramoto oscillator phase alignment.

### B. Clinical Research & Music Cognition (`docs/community/clinical/`)

- **`GETTING_STARTED.md`**: Guide for cognitive neuroscientists and clinical trial leads to design, counterbalance, and run auditory trials.
- **`IRB_TEMPLATES.md`**: High-level map explaining how to structure clinical study definitions, configure SPL headphones calibration, and deploy adverse event tracking.
- **`PUBLISHING.md`**: Steps to achieve gold-standard reproducibility (Nature, JNeurosci) by utilizing the v7.5 self-contained zip exporter, differential privacy calibrations, and validation hashes.
- **`CASE_STUDIES.md`**: Anonymized/hypothetical clinical trials: biofeedback-driven respiratory pacing (HRV/Breath) and response-latency auditory fatigue trackers.

## 2. Partnership Templates Content Outlines

A collection of five institutional drafts under `docs/templates/` designed as drop-in starting points for principal investigators (PIs) and compliance offices:

1. **`IRB_CONSENT_TEMPLATE.md`**: Informed consent boilerplates declaring exactly what telemetry is logged (acoustic features, surveys, latencies), GDPR rights, and data-shredding mechanisms.
2. **`DATA_MANAGEMENT_PLAN.md`**: Structured, NIH/NSF-compliant DMP clarifying local sandboxing, Web Bluetooth/HID privacy boundaries, and offline zip package archival.
3. **`COLLABORATION_AGREEMENT.md`**: Formal legal draft clarifying that AnnealMusic provides pure infrastructure without claiming IP or research co-ownership.
4. **`CITATION_GUIDE.md`**: Comprehensive formatting conventions for BibTeX, APA, and Chicago styles, covering engine versions, mapping matrices, and Zenodo DOI registry.
5. **`SUPPLEMENTARY_MATERIALS_GUIDE.md`**: Standards and checklists for what files (study manifests, calibration offset logs, user scripts) should be uploaded as peer-review supplements.

## 3. Case Studies Selection & Anonymization

To preserve confidentiality and build credibility, case studies are designed using anonymized or realistic hypothetical studies representing active research fields:

- **ICAD Case 1 (Magnetosphere)**: Anonymized space physics study translating interplanetary magnetic fields into spectral FM synthesis.
- **ICAD Case 2 (Canopy Acoustics)**: Real-time environmental tracking mapping temperature, humidity, and rainfall to granular textures in public ecological art.
- **ICAD Case 3 (Network Sync)**: Real-time cyber threat monitor mapping routing logs to Kuramoto coupling coefficients, making denial-of-service attempts audible via synchronization transitions.
- **Clinical Case 1 (Respiratory Entrainment)**: Evaluating calm breathing pacing using live HRV/breath rate biofeedback loops.
- **Clinical Case 2 (Auditory Fatigue)**: Assessing reaction times and headache symptoms during continuous 60-minute ambient synthesis trials.

## 4. Honest Legal Posture

The collaboration template is explicitly structured to establish a pure-infrastructure posture:

- **No Claims on Data**: All subject data, session logs, and analysis remain the sole property of the PI's institution.
- **No Default Co-authorship**: The creators of AnnealMusic do not assert co-authorship rights for academic papers utilizing the software. Citation is the standard of academic integrity.
- **Zero-Egress by Default**: Subject data never reaches AnnealMusic hosted infrastructure unless the PI explicitly configures and opts into an enterprise-hosted storage environment.

## 5. Retrospective Outline

The retrospective in `docs/V7_RETROSPECTIVE.md` will address:

1. **The original v7 thesis**: I-narrow (focusing strictly on non-therapeutic research tooling) vs. K equal-weight across synthesis/research, serving four core personas.
2. **A chronological review** of what was built (v7.0 through v7.7).
3. **An honest assessment**: Which research communities does v7.7 serve exceptionally well? (ICAD, Music Cognition, Auditory Psyche). Which need further tooling? (Clinical therapeutic interventions).
4. **Lessons from research-adjacent domains**: The complexity of SPL calibration, multi-modal biofeedback ingestion latency, and human-subjects compliance boundaries.
5. **The I-narrow vs. I-wide debate**: Did the strict scoping of the tool prevent legal/regulatory overhead (FDA medical device claims)? (Yes, it did). Should v8 revisit it?
6. **The post-v7 thesis space**: Evaluating future horizons (Eurorack integration, shared listening rooms, user-uploaded engines, concert labels, or federated research networks).

## 6. Stability Commitments Across v7 APIs

We document formal API stability commitments in `docs/STABILITY.md` to assure investigators that studies initiated in v7 will run unmodified for years:

- **Data Schemas**: `studies`, `study_resources`, `accessibility_descriptions`, and `lesson_progress` schemas are locked.
- **Python Sandbox Bindings**: The custom `anneal` module, its ast-scanning imports, and virtual matplotlib plotting WebAgg interfaces remain backwards-compatible.
- **Datalogger Format**: The CSV flattener and JSONL formats are finalized and frozen.
- **OSC Address Space**: Bidirectional namespaces (`/anneal/state`, `/anneal/engine`) are stable.

---

## 7. Risks and Mitigations

- **Risk 1: Legal/IRB Guidance Creep**: PIs might interpret templates as certified legal documents.
  - _Mitigation_: Every template carries a bold, prominent, standardized disclaimer at the top clarifying that drafts are starting points and require local legal/IRB review.
- **Risk 2: Partner Cooperation for Real Case Studies**: Relying on partners for permission might stall release.
  - _Mitigation_: Fully anonymized synthesis models and realistic hypothetical setups are used, allowing immediate release without legal friction.
